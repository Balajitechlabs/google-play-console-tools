## Description

Icon generator (22 sizes), screenshot validator, & aspect ratio converter for preparing Android apps for Google Play Console. Batch processing support, optional Google Cloud Storage integration.

## Why Submitted

Helps developers prepare app icons and screenshots efficiently for Google Play Console submissions.

## Features Submitted

- **Icon Generator:** Generates 22 icon sizes for all Android devices
- **Screenshot Validator:** Validates format, size, aspect ratio, dimensions
- **Screenshot Converter:** Fixes aspect ratios and resizes screenshots
- **Batch Processing:** Process multiple files at once
- **GCS Integration:** Optional Google Cloud Storage support
- **MIT License:** Completely free and open source
